## Vision

### 10x Check
The 10x version is a **family memory platform** — restoration is the entry point, but the product becomes the place families go to preserve, organize, share, and celebrate their visual history. iCloud Photos meets Ancestry meets a professional photo lab. Every feature creates a compounding loop: restore → caption → share → cousin uploads theirs → print order → physical product with logo. Each step deepens the relationship and increases LTV. The platform vision also includes a developer API (photography studios, print shops integrate), genealogy platform partnerships (Ancestry has 4M+ paying subscribers in the exact demographic), and a community preset marketplace.

### Platonic Ideal
The app feels like a **museum for your family**. Photos aren't files — they're artifacts. The UI is warm, unhurried, and tactile. Uploading a photo feels like handing something precious to a skilled conservator. The result comes back ceremony-ified — a before/after reveal, an estimated era, a suggested caption. Sharing a restored photo feels like giving a gift, not clicking download. Print ordering is one tap. Family members can all access a shared album. The logo is on the back of every print.

## Accepted Scope (added to v1 plan)
- Before/after interactive drag slider on results page with social share CTA
- AI photo era estimation + auto-preset suggestion on upload (via OpenRouter vision model)
- Gift a Restoration flow (Stripe gifting + email delivery to recipient)
- Batch processing: upload up to 10 photos, parallel processing, zip download (Upstash QStash async)
- Admin analytics dashboard: restorations/day, revenue/day, top users, preset popularity, failed job log

## Architecture Decisions (from CEO review)
- Auth gate: anonymous upload + watermarked preview; Google OAuth required at purchase
- Image storage: Vercel Blob (migrate to R2 if costs grow)
- Batch processing: Upstash QStash async queue (not parallel fan-out — avoids Vercel timeout)
- Image retention: 30 days auto-delete; user can re-download within window
- Watermark: server-side burned-in (not CSS overlay)
- Free tier: watermarked preview only (no free credits on signup)
- Vision model for era estimation: OpenRouter cost-efficient model (TBD, separate from kie.ai)
